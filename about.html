life in color

The goal of the life in color project was to discover more about the journey of Vincent van Gogh by combining art history, quotes from his letters, and data science.
The list of artworks from WikiPedia was consulted to collect information about his art, draw statistics, and calculate the most dominant colors from the featured pictures of his artworks. The background information is retrieved from the Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam.
